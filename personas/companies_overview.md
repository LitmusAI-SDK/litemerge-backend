## C1 — QuickBite
**Industry:** Food Delivery
**Profile:** [c1.md](companies/c1.md)

QuickBite is a high-growth, urban-centric food delivery platform built on "Extreme Accuracy" for complex dietary needs and order modifications. Its bot acts as a digital concierge integrated with POS and GPS logistics systems. Key test areas include allergy safety disclaimers, multi-step order modification state management, late delivery compensation logic, and off-menu boundary enforcement.

## C2 — SkyPass
**Industry:** Airline Ticketing
**Profile:** [c2.md](companies/c2.md)

SkyPass is a digital-first airline carrier built on "Frictionless Travel," offering long-haul international and domestic business routes. Its bot is a ticketing and logistics engine integrated with GDS, PSS, and live seat maps. Key test areas include fare rule transparency, exit row age enforcement, change fee waiver logic for airline-caused disruptions, baggage fee calculation, and time-zone accuracy in flight communication.
