## C1 — QuickBite
**Industry:** Food Delivery
**Profile:** [c1.md](companies/c1.md)

QuickBite is a high-growth, urban-centric food delivery platform built on "Extreme Accuracy" for complex dietary needs and order modifications. Its bot acts as a digital concierge integrated with POS and GPS logistics systems. Key test areas include allergy safety disclaimers, multi-step order modification state management, late delivery compensation logic, and off-menu boundary enforcement.

## C2 — SkyPass
**Industry:** Airline Ticketing
**Profile:** [c2.md](companies/c2.md)

SkyPass is a digital-first airline carrier built on "Frictionless Travel," offering long-haul international and domestic business routes. Its bot is a ticketing and logistics engine integrated with GDS, PSS, and live seat maps. Key test areas include fare rule transparency, exit row age enforcement, change fee waiver logic for airline-caused disruptions, baggage fee calculation, and time-zone accuracy in flight communication.

---

## C3 — ClearBank
**Industry:** Retail Banking
**Profile:** [c3.md](companies/c3.md)

ClearBank is a digital-first retail bank built on "Proactive Security," operating under a Zero-Trust framework with MFA enforcement for all sensitive actions. Its bot handles account inquiries, fund transfers, fraud disputes, and loan queries. Key test areas include data masking (PII), MFA bypass resistance, card freeze and dispute initiation, red-flag escalation for blackmail/scam mentions, and groundedness under gaslighting attempts.

---

## C4 — CareDesk
**Industry:** Healthcare / Patient Intake
**Profile:** [c4.md](companies/c4.md)

CareDesk is a HIPAA/GDPR-compliant health-tech platform built on "Intelligent Triage," bridging patients and providers through an EHR-integrated intake assistant. Key test areas include emergency red-flag detection (chest pain, sudden numbness), strict no-diagnosis enforcement, PHI handling, multi-patient scheduling state management, and deflection of out-of-scope medical advice requests.

---

## C5 — ShopBot
**Industry:** E-Commerce / Retail
**Profile:** [c5.md](companies/c5.md)

ShopBot is the AI commerce assistant for a global multi-brand retail conglomerate focused on "Hyper-Personalized Retail." Its bot is integrated with inventory, shipping carriers, and purchase history. Key test areas include out-of-stock filtering, size conversion accuracy, Final Sale policy enforcement, lost package resolution flow, promo code discount logic, and style-preference consistency across recommendations.
