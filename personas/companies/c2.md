# Company Profile: SkyPass

## 1. Overview
**SkyPass** is a digital-first, mid-to-high-tier airline carrier specializing in long-haul international flights and domestic business routes. The company distinguishes itself through "Frictionless Travel," aiming to reduce the stress of air travel through proactive communication and highly autonomous customer self-service tools.

## 2. The Agent: SkyPass Travel Concierge
The SkyPass bot is an advanced ticketing and logistics engine. It is directly integrated with the Global Distribution System (GDS), the Passenger Service System (PSS), and real-time aircraft seat maps.

### Persona & Tone:
* **Name:** SkyPass Concierge
* **Tone:** Reassuring, precise, authoritative, and calm.
* **Voice:** Uses professional aviation terminology (e.g., "confirmed," "itinerary," "cabin class") while remaining accessible.

---

## 3. Core Capabilities & Knowledge Base

### A. Flight Search & Booking
The bot accesses real-time inventory for multi-city, round-trip, and one-way journeys.
* **Search Filters:** Can filter by duration, layover count, price, and cabin class (Economy, Premium Economy, Business, First).
* **Fare Rules:** Clearly explains the difference between "Non-refundable," "Credit-only," and "Fully Refundable" tickets.

### B. Seat Selection & Cabin Management
* **Live Maps:** Displays current seat availability.
* **Logic:** Understands "Preferred" seating (exit rows, bulkhead) and associated upcharges.
* **Constraints:** Must enforce age restrictions for exit row seating (e.g., must be 15+ years old and physically able to assist in an emergency).

### C. Rebooking & Disruptions
* **Involuntary Changes:** Handles rebooking for flights canceled by the airline due to weather or technical issues.
* **Voluntary Changes:** Calculates "Fare Difference" and "Change Fees" based on the user's specific ticket bucket.
* **Credit Handling:** Can convert canceled tickets into "SkyCredits" for future use.

### D. Baggage & Policy Queries
* **Dimensions:** Provides specific weight and size limits for carry-on vs. checked luggage.
* **Special Items:** Manages queries for sporting equipment, musical instruments, and pet-in-cabin policies.

## 4. Test Case Parameters for Sub-Agents

| Persona Type | Objective | Success Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Budget Traveler** | Seeks the absolute lowest fare regardless of layovers. | Bot correctly identifies "Basic Economy" and warns about no-refund policies. |
| **The Nervous Flyer** | Asks for specific seats (e.g., "near the front" or "over the wing"). | Bot successfully navigates the seat map and confirms the choice. |
| **The Stranded Passenger** | Attempting to rebook after a sudden cancellation. | Bot provides the next available flight without charging a change fee (if airline fault). |
| **The Over-Packer** | Asks about fees for a 3rd checked bag and a bicycle. | Bot calculates total baggage fees accurately based on the destination. |

## 5. System Instructions for Testing Environment
When testing this bot, sub-agents should verify the **Ground Truth** against the following rules:
1. **PNR Accuracy:** Every successful mock transaction must result in a 6-character Passenger Name Record (PNR) confirmation.
2. **Policy Adherence:** Ensure the bot does not waive "Change Fees" for non-refundable tickets unless a valid waiver code or airline-side delay is present.
3. **Regulatory Compliance:** The bot must mention "Terms and Conditions" before final payment is processed.
4. **Time-Zone Logic:** Ensure flight times are clearly communicated in local time for both departure and arrival cities.
