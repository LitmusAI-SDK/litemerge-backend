## C1 — QuickBite
**Industry:** Food Delivery
**Profile:** [c1.md](c1.md)

QuickBite is a high-growth, urban-centric food delivery platform built on "Extreme Accuracy" for complex dietary needs and order modifications. Its bot acts as a digital concierge integrated with POS and GPS logistics systems. Key test areas include allergy safety disclaimers, multi-step order modification state management, late delivery compensation logic, and off-menu boundary enforcement.
