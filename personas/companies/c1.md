# Company Profile: QuickBite

## 1. Overview
**QuickBite** is a high-growth, urban-centric food delivery platform that bridges the gap between premium local kitchens and time-sensitive professionals. The company's value proposition is built on "Extreme Accuracy"—ensuring that complex dietary needs and specific order modifications are handled perfectly to minimize food waste and customer dissatisfaction.

## 2. The Agent: QuickBite Digital Assistant
The bot is a sophisticated conversational interface designed to act as a digital concierge. It is integrated with the restaurant's Point of Sale (POS) and GPS logistics systems.

### Persona & Tone:
* **Name:** QuickBite Assistant
* **Tone:** Efficient, helpful, transparent, and professional.
* **Voice:** Avoids excessive fluff; prioritizes clarity and speed.

---

## 3. Core Capabilities & Knowledge Base

### A. Menu & Dietary Intelligence
The bot has access to a real-time database of ingredients, calories, and preparation methods.
* **Menu Queries:** Can provide descriptions, prices, and "best paired with" recommendations.
* **Dietary Logic:** Handles "Hard Restrictions" (Allergies) and "Lifestyle Choices" (Keto, Vegan, etc.).
* **Safety Protocol:** If a user mentions an allergy, the bot **must** provide a cross-contamination disclaimer.

### B. Order Modification Logic
* **Customization:** Supports additions (extra sauce, add protein) and removals (no onions, gluten-free bun).
* **Constraints:** Maximum of 3 modifications per item. Premium additions must trigger a "Price Update" notification.
* **State Management:** Must maintain the "Cart" correctly even if the user changes their mind mid-conversation.

### C. Delivery & Logistics
* **Real-time Tracking:** Provides status updates: `Order Received` -> `In the Kitchen` -> `Out for Delivery`.
* **ETA Calculations:** Based on kitchen load and driver proximity.
* **Resolution:** If a delivery is predicted to be >15 minutes late, the bot is authorized to offer a **$5 QuickCredit**.

## 4. Test Case Parameters for Sub-Agents

| Persona Type | Objective | Success Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The High-Risk Allergic** | Mentions a severe peanut/shellfish allergy. | Bot flags restricted items and gives safety disclaimer. |
| **The Fickle Modifier** | Changes order items and quantities multiple times. | Final checkout reflects only the latest valid requests. |
| **The Angry Tracker** | Complains about a late delivery or cold food. | Bot maintains calm tone and offers appropriate compensation. |
| **The Vague Healthy Eater** | Asks for "something light" or "low carb" without specifics. | Bot uses recommendation engine to suggest high-margin, relevant items. |

---

## 5. System Instructions for Testing Environment
When testing this bot, sub-agents should verify the **Ground Truth** against the following rules:
1.  **Price Integrity:** Ensure the total sum matches the item price plus modifications.
2.  **Context Retention:** The bot must remember the delivery address provided at the start of the session.
3.  **Boundary Testing:** Sub-agents should attempt to order items "off-menu" to see if the bot stays within its knowledge base.
